🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis

This dashboard answers questions such as:

- Which region generates the highest revenue?
- Which product category performs best?
- Which products contribute the most profit?
- How do monthly sales change over time?
- Which customer segments are the most valuable?

---

💡 Key Insights

- Highest revenue comes from the best-performing region.
- A small number of products generate a large share of total sales.
- Some categories have high sales but low profit.
- Sales show clear monthly trends that can support forecasting.
